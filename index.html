One of the well-known printer brands that provides outstanding printing quality at relatively affordable pricing is HP. For both home and business use, these printers can produce stunning prints. Download HP Printer Driver- 123.hp.com


You must adhere to the procedures provided below in order to obtain the HP printer driver from 123.hp.com:
  1. Start by launching a web browser on your computer, such as Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Safari, etc.
  2. Then, in the address bar that is at the top of your screen, type 123.hp.com. Press the Enter key on your keyboard after that.
  3. Choose your printer from the website's upper panel, which includes Envy, Officejet, Laserjet, and Deskjet.
  4. Enter the model of your printer into the search form now, and then click the Search button that appears.
  5. The driver will begin downloading when you click the Download button in the following window.
  6. The downloaded file will be stored automatically in the Downloads folder. However, you must do so manually if you wish to store the file in another folder.

How can an HP wireless printer be reinstalled?


Follow these step-by-step steps to remove your HP printer software from your operating system and then reinstall it:
  1. Type "Devices and Printers" when the Windows operating system first launches.
  2. Select the HP wireless printer software by searching for it, right-clicking, and selecting the 'Delete or Remove' option.
  3. Run the command by simultaneously pressing the Windows and R keys.
  4. Now, enter "printui.exe/s" in the "Search Bar" and press "Ok."
  5. Open the "HP Printer Driver" and choose "Remove" if it is recognized.
  6. Delete every additional instance of the HP printer software.
  7. Before restarting the operating system, close all open tabs.
  8. Download and reinstall your HP wireless printer using the 123.hp.com/setup website after the PC has finally started.
